Our History

What to know about us !

Helping Hands started in 2022 as a result of the need to give hope to a better future and a means of finding a solution to Sustainable Development Goal 1 whose agenda is to end poverty i.e No Poverty. This initiative was founded by a group of tech women from Africa to find a solution and a means to eradicate and help shape the world to a better and beautiful place where children live happily, graduates secure better opportunities, great employable skills are learnt, connections made, education is valued and in all poverty is eradicated. According to a research, poverty can be said to be a state or condition in which a person or community lacks financial resources and essentials to enjoy a minimum standard of life and well-being that's considered acceptable in the society.Some of these causes may include lack of education, lack of relevant skills, lack of food and social injustice just to mention a few which are a stepping stone to poverty. This was considered and hence this site was developed to help address the Sustainable Development Goal 1 of the UnitedNations that has overwhelmed Africa and the rest of the world.
